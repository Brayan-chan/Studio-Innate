If you’re looking for a way to showcase your work in a more engaging and visually appealing way, then this Walking 3D Oversized T-Shirt mockup for Blender is the perfect solution. With just a few simple clicks, you can add your own branding or design and animate it to bring your concepts to life. This mockup is also great for creating promotional videos or social media content.

Customising this mockup couldn’t be easier, making it ideal for first-time Blender (free software) users and time-savvy streetwear brands and designers. In just a couple of clicks, you can place your artwork in any orientation you choose. Just sit back and let the mockup automatically warp and distort your design to inherit the natural light and fabric ripples. And of course, you can adjust the garment, scene and lighting to anything you like.

Software: Prebuilt and fully functional 3D Blender file
Easy to use: Well organised project file with fully customisable nodes.
Lighting: Switch (on/off) and change lighting and environment colours
Animation: Smooth 360 animation included
Colour: Set any background scene, environment, product and print colours to anything you like
Garment Effects (NEW): Screen Print / Puff Print / Embroidery / Vintage Wash
